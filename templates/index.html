Mission to Mars

This page compiles data related to the mission to mars at the push of the provided button.

Data scraped includes:

  • Latest Mars News: The latest News Title and Paragragh Text from the NASA Mars News Site.
  • Current Weather on Mars: The Current weather from the Mars Weather twitter account.
  • Featured Mars Image: JPL's Featured Space Image from jpl.nasa.gov.
  • Mars Facts: The Mars Planer Profile from space-facts.com.
  • Mars Hemispheres: High resolution images of each of Mars' hemispheres from the USGS Astrogeology site.
